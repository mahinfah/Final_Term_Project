DETAILS:
Role 3 — Receptionist
Description
Receptionists handle the physical front-desk experience of the hospital. They book walk-in appointments, manage the daily check-in queue, process payments, and search patient and doctor records to assist anyone who arrives at the hospital.
Features
•	Log in and manage profile; view the hospital's full daily appointment schedule at a glance
•	View all appointments for all doctors for today: grouped by doctor, sortable by time, filterable by status
•	Search patients by name, phone number, or patient ID; view their profile, upcoming appointments, and billing status
•	Register a new patient into the system (enter their full information) on their behalf
•	Book an appointment for a walk-in patient: select doctor, confirm an available slot via AJAX, and enter reason; overrides normal booking flow
•	Check patients in when they arrive: search appointment, confirm patient identity, update status to Checked In
•	View the current waiting room queue for each doctor (all Checked-In patients waiting)
•	Process appointment payments: view the outstanding bill for a completed appointment, mark it as paid, record payment method
•	Generate a payment receipt (formatted printable view) for a processed payment
•	Cancel or reschedule an appointment on a patient's behalf with a recorded reason
•	View doctor availability across all doctors for a given date — used to advise walk-in patients on who can see them soonest
•	Generate a daily appointment summary report: total appointments, check-ins, completions, cancellations, and revenue collected
